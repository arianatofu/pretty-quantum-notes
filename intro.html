Pretty Quantum Notes

bonjour friends! I'm arianatofu ♡

I made this little quantum notebook because when I first started learning quantum mechanics, it honestly felt really overwhelming. The math, the abstract ideas, and the way everything suddenly stops behaving like normal physics can make it feel very confusing at first.

While studying, I thought it might help to have a cute, organized reference where I could collect explanations, important equations, and examples all in one place. Something that feels a little less intimidating than a dense textbook and more like flipping through a cozy notebook.

So this site is my attempt to build that. Here you'll find notes covering topics from Quantum Mechanics I and II, starting with the basics and working toward more advanced ideas.

If you're learning quantum too and sometimes feel confused or stuck, you're definitely not alone. Hopefully this notebook can be a small reference you can flip through when things start getting weird (which in quantum… happens a lot).
